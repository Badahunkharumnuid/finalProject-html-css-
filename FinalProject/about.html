Welcome to the Beautiful Places in Meghalaya: living root Bridges

Single-decker Living Root Bridge in Shillong

The bridges can be hundreds of feet long and are incredibly strong, able to support the weight of dozens of people at a time. They are also incredibly beautiful, with a natural, organic look that is unlike any other type of bridge in the world.
